Salavat Shcherbakov

National Artist of Russia
"Sculptor Andrey Sledkov worked in our creative team for a long time. He showed himself as a professional and literate artist. Andrey is an interesting and creative person. He has great prospects for development and work as a sculptor"

Andrey Sledkov is a famous, Moscow-based Russian sculptor and the creator of many monuments.

Andrey graduated in 2010 from the Moscow Art Institute named after Surikov. When he was just 23 years old, he established his first monument in the Russian capital.

A year later, Andrey joined the Moscow Union of Artists and the Moscow Union of Sculptors. He took part in the Russian ART Week on many occasions.
